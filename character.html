Your Name (Kimi no Na wa)

A visually stunning story of two teenagers who mysteriously swap bodies and unravel a cosmic tie that binds them.

Introduction

Your Name features characters connected through time, memory, and fate. The main focus is on two teenager's whose lives interwine through a mysterious body-swapping phenomenon.

Mitsuha Miyamizu

A high school girl from Itomori who yearns for city life but dreams of something greater.

Taki Tachibana

An optimistic Tokyo-based student who experiences life in Itomori through Mitsuha's eyes.

Protagonists

Mitsuha Miyamizu

A high school girl living in the rural town of Itomori. She is tired of her traditional lifestyle and dreams of living in Tokyo.

Voice Actor: Mone Kamishiraishi
Taki Tachibana

A high school boy living in Tokyo. He is hardworking, kind, and has a part-time job at an Italian restaurant.

Voice Actor: Ryunosuke Kamiki
Miki Okudera

Taki's kind and mature co-worker. She initially has a crush on Taki and joins him in his search for Mitsuha.

Voice Actor: Masami Nagasawa
Tessie (Katsuhiko Tashigawara)

One of Mitsuha's childhood friends. He is knowledgable about tools and explosives and later helps evacuate the town.

Voice Actor: Ryo Narita
Sayaka Natori

Mitsuha's cheerful and loyal friend. She supports Tessie and Mitsuha during the comet crisis.

Voice Actor: Aoi Yuki

Antagonists

The Comet (Tiamat)

A celestial comet that causes the catastrophic event in Itomori.

Role: Acts as the force that seperates the characters through time and tragedy.
Time Gap / Fate

The timeline differences between Taki and Mitsuha serve as a major obstacle, preventing them from meeting.

Role: Separation of time, fate and memory.
